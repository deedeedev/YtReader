<p>YtReader turns YouTube into a focused reading experience. Search for videos, follow subtitles with synchronized highlighting, and save the parts that matter.</p>

<p><b>Features</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Browse and search YouTube videos</li>
  <li>Read subtitles with synchronized word highlighting</li>
  <li>AI-powered subtitle text cleaning (OpenAI-compatible API, bring your own key)</li>
  <li>Organize videos into collections</li>
  <li>Highlight and annotate text</li>
  <li>Adjustable font, size, and line height for comfortable reading</li>
  <li>Light, dark, and system themes</li>
  <li>Home screen widget for quick access to your library</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Privacy</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>No account required</li>
  <li>No tracking or analytics</li>
  <li>AI features are opt-in and use your own API key</li>
  <li>All data stored locally on your device</li>
</ul>
